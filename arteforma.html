About

(01)

( The client )
Arterorma, a luxury interior design salon showcasing unique furniture from all over the world
( The task )
Design and develop a multi-page website to present the new business, introduce the range or brands they carry, and invite the users to visit the showroom

Key ideas

(02)

01
(Focus on comfort and quality)
Arteforma showcases items from the world's leading luxury brands. To convey their quality and timelessness, we created a suitable atmosphere on the website by using subtle typography, large product images, gentle decorative lines, and smooth animations.
02
(Custom catalog)
To enhance the impression on the visually experienced clients, our brand catalog features horizontal scroll, complemented by custom pictograms for categories. We also provide the option to switch to regular grid view to ease interaction if needed.
03
(Convenience for users and the client)
The brand pages support the style of the website, while also featuring user-friendly solutions: large margins for comfortable reading, navigation for continued exploration. The client is planning brand updates in the future, so the pages were made easy to manipulate without our help.
( The result )
The website has been featured in the selection of finest projects on Tilda platfrom, and is helping Arteforma attract their target audience through an impressive web presence
